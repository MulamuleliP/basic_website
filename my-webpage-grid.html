Historical places

Gold Reef City

It is a theme park built around an old gold mine, offering both entertainment and educational experiences about Johannesburg's gold rush era.There's an underground mine, visitors can take an underground tour of the mine, descending 75 meters below the surface to see what life was like for the miners.

Mandela House

It is a former Residence of Nelson Mandela located in Soweto, Mandela House is the former home of Nelson Mandela, where he lived from 1946 to 1962 before his imprisonment.The house has been preserved as a museum, showcasing Mandela's personal memorabilia and providing insights into his life and legacy.

Hector Pieterson Memorial and Museum

The museum features exhibits with photographs, oral histories, and personal testimonies that detail the events of the Soweto Uprising and its impact on the apartheid regime.The memorial and museum commemorate the events of June 16, 1976, when thousands of students protested against the imposition of Afrikaans as a medium of instruction, leading to a brutal crackdown by police
